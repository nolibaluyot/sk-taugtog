Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Taugtog

The Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Taugtog participate in Sangguniang Kabataan Mandatory Training 2023.

  • SKMT stands for Sangguniang Kabataan Mandatory Training. It is a required program in the Philippines for newly elected officials of the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK), which translates to "Youth Council" in English.

    Governance and Leadership
    Rights and responsibilities of SK Officials
    Project Development and Management
    Community Organizing

    Upon completion of the SKMT, new SK officials can then take their oath of office and begin their term of service.

  • The SKMT aims to equip the incoming SK officials with the proper skills and knowledge to effectively do their duties and responsibilities as representatives of the youth. SK Mandatory Training is intended to emphasize the role of the youth in nation-building and molding them to become better citizens with the values of patriotism, nationalism and honor as Filipino.

  • The SKMT consists of three modules --

    Module 1 which talks about Decentralization and Local Governance, SK History and Salient Features;

    Module 2 which includes Meetings and Resolutions, Planning and Budgeting; and

    Module 3 Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards.

    All modules were carefully crafted and aimed at making the newly elected SK official be equipped with the proper tools he/she will need to perform his/her functions and duties as newly elected SK officials.
